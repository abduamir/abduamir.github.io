    Our Partners

    Phoenix products are also available through our partners. We're all on your team.

    ALCOR Scientific

    ALCOR Scientific, founded in 2001, is a U.S.-based company specializing in the design and manufacture of innovative diagnostic instruments and medical devices. Their products, including the iSED® line of Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate analyzers and the Sentinel enteral feeding pumps, aim to modernize traditional laboratory processes, enhancing efficiency and cost-effectiveness. All equipment is designed, manufactured, and supported in their Smithfield, Rhode Island facility, with distribution worldwide.

    Learn More

    Fujirebio

    Fujirebio, established in 1950 and headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, is a global leader in in vitro diagnostics (IVD). The company specializes in developing, manufacturing, and marketing high-quality diagnostic testing solutions, with a strong focus on neurodegenerative diseases. Fujirebio operates eight manufacturing facilities worldwide, producing over 250 million tests annually, and maintains a dedicated R&D team of over 300 employees. As a member of the H.U. Group Holdings Inc., Fujirebio continues to pioneer innovative diagnostic technologies and biomarkers, contributing significantly to global healthcare advancements.

    Learn More

    CS Medical

    CS Medical LLC, founded in 2003 and based in Creedmoor, North Carolina, specializes in developing, manufacturing, and marketing medical devices designed for the cleaning and high-level disinfection of ultrasound probes. Their product line includes automated systems like the Ethos® and TEEClean® cleaners, which streamline the reprocessing of transvaginal, transrectal, transesophageal (TEE), and surface ultrasound probes. These innovations aim to minimize patient risk associated with improperly reprocessed equipment, ensuring compliance with healthcare standards.
