      ALCOR Scientific

      Fujirebio

      Fujirebio, established in 1950 and headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, is a global leader in in vitro diagnostics (IVD). The company specializes in developing, manufacturing, and marketing high-quality diagnostic testing solutions, with a strong focus on neurodegenerative diseases. Fujirebio operates eight manufacturing facilities worldwide, producing over 250 million tests annually, and maintains a dedicated R&D team of over 300 employees. As a member of the H.U. Group Holdings Inc., Fujirebio continues to pioneer innovative diagnostic technologies and biomarkers, contributing significantly to global healthcare advancements.

      CS Medical

      CS Medical LLC, founded in 2003 and based in Creedmoor, North Carolina, specializes in developing, manufacturing, and marketing medical devices designed for the cleaning and high-level disinfection of ultrasound probes. Their product line includes automated systems like the Ethos¬Æ and TEEClean¬Æ cleaners, which streamline the reprocessing of transvaginal, transrectal, transesophageal (TEE), and surface ultrasound probes. These innovations aim to minimize patient risk associated with improperly reprocessed equipment, ensuring compliance with healthcare standards.

      Siemens Healthineers

      Siemens Healthineers is a leading medical technology company with over 120 years of experience and 18,500 patents globally. The company is headquartered in Erlangen, Germany, and provides innovative solutions in diagnostic and therapeutic imaging, laboratory diagnostics, and molecular medicine. Siemens Healthineers aims to improve patient care and enable healthcare providers to deliver high-quality, efficient services.
