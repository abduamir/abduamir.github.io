      Radgil2 X-ray Blood Irradiator

      For safe and effective X-ray irradiation of blood and blood components.

      RADGIL2 is a self-contained X-ray irradiator specifically designed for the irradiation of blood and blood products. It is well-suited for the prevention of Transfusion-associated Graft-versus-Host Disease (TA-GvHD), a rare but serious complication of blood transfusion, and serves as a safer, non-radioisotope alternative to traditional Cesium-137 sourced irradiators.

      The system is safe, easy to use, and requires no specialized training or radioprotection measures. Adjustable parameters also make RADGIL2 ideal for a range of research applications, including:

      • Stem cell and tissue irradiations
      • Vaccine irradiations
      • Bone marrow ablation on laboratory animals
      • High Dose Rate: for fast irradiation times
      • High Throughput: holds up to six blood products per cycle
      • Rotating Canister: provides uniform dose distribution throughout the X-ray cycle
      • Dose Calibration & Integrated Data management System
      • Safe and cost effective alternative to Cesium
      • Health Canada and FDA Cleared for use in blood irradiation
